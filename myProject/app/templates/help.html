Help & Information

How to Use the Library

Welcome to the Spartan Library platform! This guide will help you understand how to navigate our site and make the most of our resources.

  • Navigating the Site: Use the side navigation bar to access different sections of the library, such as book inventory, your checked-out books, and your profile.
  • Checking Out Books: To check out a book, go to the 'Book Inventory' page, select a book, and click on 'Check Out' if it's available.
  • Returning Books: To return a book, go to 'My Books' or 'Browse Books' and click on 'Return' next to the book you wish to return.

About Our Library

Our library is dedicated to providing students and guests with a wide range of books across different genres and subjects. We aim to support learning and development through reading.

  • Vast Collection: We offer a variety of books, from academic texts to fiction and non-fiction.
  • Community Engagement: Our library is a community space for learning, discussion, and sharing ideas.
